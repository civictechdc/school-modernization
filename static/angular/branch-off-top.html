Funding a Growing Charter Sector: Charter Facilities Allowance Add-on

Starting in 1999 the city provided a per-student appropriation to ensure charter school students could be in adequate facilities. The Facilities Allowance add-on is for leasing or purchasing buildings and capital construction. Costs for facilities cleaning, utilities, maintenance and minor repairs are already included in the per student funding formula.

From 1999 - 2015, charter operators (private governed non-profits) received a total of $977 million from the city in school facilities allowance add-ons. Currently this is $3,124 annually per day student and $8,395 for residential students.
