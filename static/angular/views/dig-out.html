DCPS digs out: $1 billion to stabilize neglected DCPS facilities


In 1995, DCPS facilities were an average of 65 years old with $1.2 billion (adjusted to reflect 2015 inflation) of deferred maintenance and repairs.


From FY1998 to FY2015, the City has allocated roughly $1 billion to stabilize and downsize DCPS buildings and grounds to ensure healthy and safe school facilities.
