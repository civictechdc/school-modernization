Welcome


In 1995, the District of Columbia Public Schools executed its first major investment to modernize all of the DCPS school buildings and grounds. Since then, the District has made tremendous investments in its public education facilities, but there is still work to be done.


It is our hope that the District’s leaders will use this dataset to inform and guide decisions on the proposed capital budget and charter facilities allowance add-ons for FY 2017-2022 and beyond.


What follows is a brief history of school facility spending and two tools to access data from 1998 – 2015.


This is a project brought to you by 21st Century School Fund and volunteers from Code for DC.
